# Quote Vault

**Quote Vault** lets you easily collect, organize, and save quotes from TikTok, Instagram, or images from your gallery. The app automatically extracts quotes and keeps them in one place for easy access and search.  

---

## Features

- Upload links from TikTok or Instagram.
- Upload images from your gallery.
- Automatically extract quotes from videos, posts, or images.
- Organize all quotes in one easy-to-access place.
- Search, save, and revisit your favorite quotes anytime.

---

## How to Use

1. Open the app.
2. Upload a TikTok or Instagram link, or select an image from your gallery.
3. The app extracts quotes automatically.
4. Browse, search, and save your favorite quotes.
